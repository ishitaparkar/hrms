## Unified Profile Page

The profile page has been redesigned to combine account settings and employee information in one convenient location.

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Click on **"My Space"** in the sidebar
2. Select **"Profile"**
3. You'll see your unified profile page with two tabs

### Profile Tabs

#### Tab 1: Account Settings

Manage your account security and preferences:

- **Change Password**: Update your login password
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable/disable 2FA for enhanced security
- **Login History**: View recent login activity
- **Session Management**: Manage active sessions

**How to Change Your Password:**
1. Navigate to Profile ‚Üí Account Settings tab
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click "Update Password"

**How to Enable 2FA:**
1. Navigate to Profile ‚Üí Account Settings tab
2. Scroll to the Two-Factor Authentication section
3. Click "Enable 2FA"
4. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
5. Enter the verification code
6. Save your backup codes in a safe place

#### Tab 2: Employee Profile

View and edit your employee information:

- **Contact Information**: Email, phone, address
- **Job Information**: Employee ID, designation, department, joining date
- **Assigned Assets**: Company assets assigned to you (laptop, phone, etc.)
- **Emergency Contacts**: Emergency contact information

**How to Update Contact Information:**
1. Navigate to Profile ‚Üí Employee Profile tab
2. Click the "Edit" button in the Contact Information section
3. Update your details
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Profile Header

At the top of your profile page, you'll see:
- Your profile picture/avatar
- Your full name
- Your email address
- Your role badges (e.g., "HR Manager", "Employee")

## Navigation Guide

### Quick Access to Common Tasks

#### Managing Your Personal Information

**View Your Payroll:**
1. Click "My Space" ‚Üí "Payroll"
2. View your salary slips and payment history

**Check Your Attendance:**
1. Click "My Space" ‚Üí "Attendance"
2. View your attendance records and clock in/out

**Request Leave:**
1. Click "My Space" ‚Üí "Leaves"
2. Click "Request Leave"
3. Fill in the leave details
4. Submit for approval

**Track Your Time:**
1. Click "My Space" ‚Üí "Time Tracker"
2. Start/stop timers for tasks
3. View your time logs

**View Your Assets:**
1. Click "My Space" ‚Üí "Assets"
2. See all company assets assigned to you

#### Managing Employees (HR Manager Functions)

**View All Employees:**
1. Click "Employee Management" ‚Üí "Staff Directory"
2. Browse, search, and filter employees
3. Click on an employee to view their details

**Add New Employee:**
1. Click "Employee Management" ‚Üí "Add New Staff"
2. Fill in the employee details
3. Assign role and department
4. Click "Create Employee"

**Approve Leave Requests:**
1. Click "My Space" ‚Üí "Leaves"
2. Switch to the "Pending Approvals" tab
3. Review leave requests
4. Approve or reject as needed

## UI Improvements

### Consistent Design

All pages now feature a consistent design with:

- **Standardized Headers**: Every page has a clear title and description
- **Uniform Cards**: Content is organized in clean, consistent cards
- **Consistent Buttons**: All buttons follow the same style guidelines
- **Dark Mode Support**: Seamless dark mode across all pages

### Visual Enhancements

#### Page Headers
Every page now has a professional header with:
- Large, clear title
- Descriptive subtitle
- Optional action buttons (e.g., "Add New", "Export")
- Optional icon for visual identification

#### Card Components
Content is organized in cards with:
- Clear titles and icons
- Consistent spacing and padding
- Hover effects where appropriate
- Responsive design for mobile devices

#### Button Styles
Buttons are now standardized with clear visual hierarchy:
- **Primary** (blue): Main actions (Save, Submit, Create)
- **Secondary** (gray): Alternative actions (Cancel, Back)
- **Danger** (red): Destructive actions (Delete, Remove)
- **Success** (green): Positive actions (Approve, Confirm)
- **Outline**: Subtle actions (Filter, Export)

### Dark Mode

The entire application supports dark mode:
- Automatically adapts to your system preferences
- Toggle dark mode in Settings (if available)
- Consistent colors and contrast in both modes
- Easy on the eyes for extended use

### Responsive Design

All pages are optimized for different screen sizes:
- **Mobile** (phones): Single column layout, touch-friendly buttons
- **Tablet**: Two-column layout where appropriate
- **Desktop**: Full multi-column layout with optimal spacing

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Where did "My Profile" go?

**A:** "My Profile" has been merged with "Admin Account" into a single "Profile" page under "My Space". All your information is now in one convenient location with tabs for Account Settings and Employee Profile.

### Q: How do I access my personal information now?

**A:** Click on "My Space" in the sidebar. This dropdown contains all your personal items including Profile, Payroll, Assets, Attendance, Leaves, Time Tracker, and Performance.

### Q: Where is the employee management section?

**A:** Employee management functions are now under "Employee Management" in the sidebar. This includes Staff Directory and Add New Staff.

### Q: Can I still access all the same features?

**A:** Yes! All features are still available. We've just reorganized the menu to make it easier to find what you need. Nothing has been removed.

### Q: What if I bookmarked the old "/my-profile" URL?

**A:** Old URLs are automatically redirected to the new unified profile page. Your bookmarks will still work.

### Q: How do I switch between Account Settings and Employee Profile?

**A:** On the Profile page, you'll see two tabs at the top. Click on the tab you want to view. The system remembers your last selected tab.

### Q: Why can't I see "Employee Management" in my menu?

**A:** The "Employee Management" menu is only visible to users with HR Manager or Super Admin roles. Regular employees will only see "My Space" with their personal items.

### Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?

**A:** Contact your system administrator or IT support team with details about the issue or feature request.

### Q: Is my data secure?

**A:** Yes! All security measures remain in place. The reorganization only affects the user interface, not the underlying security or data protection.

### Q: Can I customize the menu order?

**A:** Currently, the menu structure is fixed to ensure consistency across all users. Future updates may include customization options.

### Q: What browsers are supported?

**A:** The system is tested and supported on:
- Google Chrome (latest version)
- Mozilla Firefox (latest version)
- Safari (latest version)
- Microsoft Edge (latest version)
- Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Mobile)
