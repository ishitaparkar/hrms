# My Space - Employee User Guide

## Overview

My Space is your personal hub in the HRMS where you can manage your profile, view your team, track performance, monitor attendance, and manage leave requests. This guide will help you navigate and use all My Space features effectively.

## Accessing My Space

1. Log in to the HRMS
2. Look for the **My Space** section in the left sidebar
3. Click on any of the My Space menu items:
   - Profile
   - My Team
   - My Performance
   - My Attendance
   - My Leave

## Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

Your profile contains two main sections accessible via tabs:

#### Employee Profile Tab (Default)

This tab displays your employment information:

**Personal Information**
- Full name
- Employee ID
- Email address
- Phone number
- Date of birth
- Address

**Job Information**
- Department
- Designation
- Joining date
- Employment status
- Reporting manager

**Documents Section**

View and download your employment documents organized by category:

1. **Personal Documents**: ID cards, address proofs, etc.
2. **Employment Documents**: Offer letters, contracts, etc.
3. **Certificates**: Educational certificates, training certificates, etc.

**To download a document:**
1. Click on the category tab (Personal, Employment, or Certificates)
2. Find the document you need
3. Click on the document name to download

Each document shows:
- Document name
- File type (PDF, DOC, etc.)
- File size
- Upload date
- Verification status (Verified, Pending, Expired)

#### Account Settings Tab

Manage your account preferences and security:

**Change Password**
- Enter your current password
- Enter new password
- Confirm new password
- Click "Change Password"

**Notifications & Preferences**

Customize how you receive notifications:

1. **Email Notifications**: Toggle on/off to receive email alerts
2. **SMS Notifications**: Toggle on/off to receive text messages
3. **Push Notifications**: Toggle on/off to receive browser notifications

**Theme Settings**

Choose your preferred display theme:
- **Light**: Bright background, dark text
- **Dark**: Dark background, light text
- **System**: Automatically matches your device settings

Changes are saved automatically and apply immediately.

## My Attendance

Monitor your attendance and work hours.

### Attendance Summary

View key statistics for the current month:
- **Total Days**: Working days in the month
- **Present Days**: Days you were present
- **Absent Days**: Days you were absent
- **Late Days**: Days you arrived late
- **Attendance Percentage**: Your overall attendance rate

### Attendance Calendar

Visual calendar showing your attendance status:
- **Green**: Present
- **Red**: Absent
- **Yellow**: Late
- **Gray**: Weekend/Holiday

Navigate between months using the arrow buttons.

### Recent Check-In/Out

View your check-in and check-out times for the last 7 days:
- Date
- Check-in time
- Check-out time
- Total work hours
- Status

## My Leave

Manage your leave requests and view leave balance.

### Leave Balance

See your available leave for each type:
- **Casual Leave**: For personal matters
- **Sick Leave**: For medical reasons
- **Vacation Leave**: For planned holidays

Each card shows:
- Total allocated days
- Days used
- Days remaining

### Requesting Leave

**To submit a leave request:**

1. Click the **"Request Leave"** button
2. Fill out the form:
   - **Leave Type**: Select from dropdown (Casual, Sick, Vacation)
   - **Start Date**: Choose the first day of leave
   - **End Date**: Choose the last day of leave
   - **Reason**: Explain why you need leave
3. Click **"Submit Request"**
4. You'll see a success message
5. Your request will appear in the Pending Requests section

**Important Notes:**
- You cannot select past dates
- End date must be after start date
- System checks if you have sufficient leave balance
- Requests require manager approval

### Leave History

View all your past and current leave requests:
- Leave type
- Start and end dates
- Number of days
- Reason
- Status with color indicators:
  - **Yellow**: Pending approval
  - **Green**: Approved
  - **Red**: Rejected
- Applied date

### Upcoming Holidays

See public holidays for the current month to help plan your leave.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Q: My documents aren't showing**
- Refresh the page
- Check with HR if documents have been uploaded
- Ensure you're viewing the correct category tab

**Q: My theme preference isn't saving**
- Check your internet connection
- Look for the success message after changing theme
- Try logging out and back in

**Q: I can't submit a leave request**
- Ensure start date is in the future
- Verify end date is after start date
- Check if you have sufficient leave balance
- Make sure all required fields are filled

**Q: My attendance percentage seems wrong**
- Attendance is calculated for the current month only
- Weekends and holidays are not counted
- Contact HR if you believe there's an error

**Q: I don't see my team members**
- Team members are filtered by department
- If you're new, data may not be updated yet
- Contact HR to verify your department assignment
